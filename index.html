About Farmer's Aid

Farmer's Aid is an innovative IoT project designed to enhance agricultural practices through the smart use of technology. Our goal is to assist farmers in making informed decisions, improving crop yields, and optimizing resources to ensure sustainable farming.

Key Features

Real-time Monitoring

Track soil moisture, temperature, and other details in real-time.

Automated Irrigation

Automatically adjust irrigation levels based on sensor data, ensuring optimal water usage.

Data-Driven Insights

Get valuable insights and recommendations for improving crop health and yield.

Remote Management

Monitor and manage your farm remotely from anywhere, anytime.
